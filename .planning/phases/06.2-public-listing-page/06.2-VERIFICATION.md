### Human Verification Required

There are no blocking gaps requiring human verification before the phase is considered complete. The following items are recommended for visual QA:

#### 1. LockedSection title visibility

**Test:** Navigate to `/p/lafc` and observe each locked section
**Expected:** The section heading ("Sponsorship Tiers", "Audience Demographics", "Activation Formats") should be visible above the blurred box. The lock overlay should cover only the blurred content area, not the heading.
**Why human:** CSS positioning issue (overlay `absolute inset-0` anchors to `<section>` not to inner `div`) cannot be confirmed visually by static analysis alone. The heading may appear dimmed or fully covered depending on rendered height.

#### 2. Public page nav behavior

**Test:** Load `/p/lafc` and verify the navbar renders correctly
**Expected:** LandingNavbar shows at top; no app sidebar visible; LandingFooter at bottom
**Why human:** `LandingNavbar` uses `window.scrollY > window.innerHeight * 0.85` to switch from dark to light — on the public listing page (no full-screen hero), the navbar may render dark against a white page background until the user scrolls. This is a documented prototype tradeoff in the PLAN.

#### 3. 404 for invalid slugs

**Test:** Navigate to `/p/nonexistent-slug`
**Expected:** Next.js 404 page
**Why human:** Confirms `notFound()` call on line 17 of page.tsx works at runtime.
