## Files Created/Modified

## Decisions Made

- `SheetClose asChild` wrapping each `Link` — closes drawer on tap with no extra `open` state or onClick handler needed
- Desktop CTA group changed from `flex` (always visible, hidden by `hidden sm:inline-flex`) to `hidden lg:flex` — creates cleaner separation between the mobile hamburger and desktop CTAs without overlap
- `prefersReduced === true` (strict equality, not truthy) — `null` is returned by `useReducedMotion` during SSR or before media query resolves; animating normally in that case avoids a flash of visible content on initial render
- Badge `transitionDelay` values in performance-reporting updated from hardcoded `1.2s/1.35s/1.5s` to `0.5s/0.65s/0.8s` to keep them synchronised with the shorter `dur` value
